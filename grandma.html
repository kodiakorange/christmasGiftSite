Merry Christmas Grandma

A Christmas Card For a Wonderful Grandma

Dear Grandma,

I loved getting to see you in Missoula at Trenton's graduation. I love you so much, and I am so lucky to have had you as a part of my life. I have so many fond memories of Christmas's past at your house, and waking up in the morning to our stockings and blackberry pie. I hope you get to see snow in the backyard and that your chickens are happy and safe. I can't wait to see you again, but until then, Merry Christmas to my favorite grandma in the world.

With Love,
Dylan
